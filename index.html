Profile Summary

Welcome to the official website of Lucky Cruz, golfer and entrepreneur. Known for precision, passion, and perseverance on the golf course, Lucky continues to build his legacy through competitive golf and business ventures.

Mission Statement

To inspire excellence through golf while building meaningful partnerships and creating opportunities for growth in sports and business. Lucky Cruz is dedicated to elevating the game of golf while making a positive impact in communities through strategic collaborations and innovative initiatives.

Career Achievements

Tournament Excellence

International Recognition

Representing the country in international competitions and bringing home prestigious awards.

Sportsmanship Award

Recognized for outstanding sportsmanship and contribution to the spirit of golf.

Jr. Golf Career Excellence

71 Events, 2 World Jr Appearances Top 50, 32 wins, and 45 Top 10 finishes demonstrating consistent excellence.

2022 Season Highlights

Faldo USA Champion, Dubai International Championship T-1 (2nd), Curry Cup 4th, Notah 4th (64 Course Record), Qualified US Open 1st Stage, 3 Top 10's on Mini-Tours.

2023 Championship Season

Curry Cup Champion, NB3 National Champion, 6 Top 10 on Mini-Tours, Represented USA (The Boys R&A).

2024 Breakthrough Season

12 Top 10's, PGA Jr Championships appearance, Porter Cup, Tour of Mexico (GIRA) Q-School. 66 lowest round.

Youngest to Qualify - Full Status

Youngest player to ever qualify and receive full status on the Tour of Mexico (GIRA).

Debut Success

Bajio Open in Leon, MX - Cut Made, finished 28th. Competed in 6 events including El Salvador Open.

2025 Season Start

Cut Made - Colorado Open, Winner of Roger's Clark, Mini-Tour Runner-Up.

Final Stage Q-Schools

Qualified for Sunshine Tour and PGA-Australia, demonstrating readiness for international competition.
