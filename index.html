HOME

Profile Summary

Welcome to the official website of Lucky Cruz, professional golfer and entrepreneur. Known for precision, passion, and perseverance on the golf course, Lucky has established himself as a formidable presence in both professional golf and business ventures.

Mission Statement

To inspire excellence through golf while building meaningful partnerships and creating opportunities for growth in sports and business. Lucky Cruz is dedicated to elevating the game of golf while making a positive impact in communities through strategic collaborations and innovative initiatives.

Career Achievements

2023 Championship Victory

First major tournament win, showcasing exceptional skill and determination on the green.

Tournament Excellence

Consistent top finishes in professional tournaments, demonstrating world-class performance.

International Recognition

Representing the country in international competitions and bringing home prestigious awards.

Sportsmanship Award

Recognized for outstanding sportsmanship and contribution to the spirit of golf.

Jr. Golf Career Excellence

71 Events, 2 World Jr Appearances Top 50, 32 wins, and 45 Top 10 finishes demonstrating consistent excellence.

2022 Season Highlights

Faldo USA Champion, Dubai International Championship T-1 (2nd), Curry Cup 4th, Notah 4th (64 Course Record), Qualified US Open 1st Stage, 3 Top 10's on Mini-Tours.

2023 Championship Season

Curry Cup Champion, NB3 National Champion, 6 Top 10 on Mini-Tours, Represented USA (The Boys R&A).

2024 Professional Breakthrough

12 Top 10's, PGA Jr Championships appearance, Porter Cup, Professional Tour of Mexico (GIRA) Q-School OWGR Tour. 66 lowest round.

Youngest to Qualify - Full Status

Youngest player to ever qualify and receive full status on the Professional Tour of Mexico (GIRA).

Professional Debut Success

Bajio Open in Leon, MX - Cut Made, finished 28th. Competed in 6 events including El Salvador Open.

2025 Season Start

Cut Made - Colorado Open, Winner of "Roger's Clark", Mini-Tour Runner-Up.

Final Stage Q-Schools

Qualified for Sunshine Tour and PGA-Australia, demonstrating readiness for international professional competition.

MANAGEMENT

The Clover Management Group (TCMG)

The Clover Management Group represents Lucky Cruz and manages all aspects of his professional career, from tournament scheduling to business partnerships and media relations.

With decades of combined experience in sports management, TCMG provides comprehensive support and strategic guidance to ensure Lucky's success both on and off the golf course.

Partnership to Success

Learn about how TCMG's approach to career management and business development has contributed to Lucky's rise in professional golf and entrepreneurial ventures.

The collaborative partnership between Lucky and TCMG demonstrates how the right management team can elevate an athlete's career to new heights.

Philosophy

TCMG operates on principles of integrity, innovation, and excellence. The team believes in taking a holistic approach to athlete management, addressing not only professional performance but also personal growth and long-term career planning.

By focusing on sustainable success rather than short-term gains, TCMG has established a management model that prioritizes athlete well-being while maximizing potential.
